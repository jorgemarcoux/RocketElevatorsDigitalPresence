Mitsubishi Electric to Supply Elevators and Escalators for New National Assembly Building in Laos

  • September 4, 2020
    • Robert Desjardins

Mitsubishi Electric Corporation (TOKYO:6503) announced today that MELCO Elevator Lao Sole Co., Ltd. (MLAO), a subsidiary of Mitsubishi Elevator (Thailand) Co., Ltd. (MET), has secured an order to supply 14 elevators and 4 escalators for safe, comfortable and reliable vertical transportation in the new National Assembly building in Laos, which is currently under construction. The new National Assembly building is being built on the site of the former National Assembly building, which was constructed in 1991. The order reflects the high evaluation of the company's product and service quality in Laos.


This is excellent news for Laos, which has struggled for some years to stand out as one of the economic powers in the area. Mitsubishi also promised electric cars for all members of the Senate and bikes for every child over the age of 8.


Mitsubishi is also planning to invest in other democracies in South Asia, to support these countries with great potential to develop economically and politically.
